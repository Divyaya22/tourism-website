Discover the Food & Culture of J&K

where tradition meets taste in every district

Kashmiri Rogan Josh

A fragrant curry made with tender meat, saffron, and flavorful spices. A true representation of Kashmiri cuisine!

Rajma

Rajma is a popular vegetarian dish in Jammu, especially when paired with rice. The red kidney beans are slow-cooked in rich spices, making it a hearty and flavorful dish.

Dogra Cuisine (Madra)

Meetha Madra is a traditional sweet dish from Jammu, and it's made using dry fruits.

Kaladi Kulcha

Udhampur has earned a special place for this delicacy, and many locals and visitors associate the region with its iconic Kaladi.

Ambal

Ambal is a traditional dish from Jammu, known for its sweet and tangy flavor.

Gulgule

Gulgule is a traditional sweet snack from Jammu, typically made during festivals and special occasions.

Dum Aloo

Kashmir’s version of Dum Aloo features baby potatoes cooked slowly in a thick yogurt-based gravy, flavored with fennel and Kashmiri red chili powder.

Wazwan

Wazwan is a traditional Kashmiri multi-course meal, often served at weddings. It includes dishes like Tabak Maaz, Rista, and Goshtaba.

Anardana Chutney

This tangy chutney made from dried pomegranate seeds (anardana) is commonly enjoyed in the region, adding a unique flavor to various dishes.

Patisa

Kathua is known for its version of this flaky, sweet dessert, which is similar to soan papdi, but richer and crunchier.

Yakhni

Yakhni is a mild yogurt-based lamb curry, seasoned with cardamom, cloves, and fennel seeds, making it rich in flavor but lighter on spices.

Haak Saag

A popular vegetarian dish, Haak is made with green leafy vegetables (collard greens or spinach) cooked simply with a few spices and oil.

Kashmiri Phiran

Phiran, a traditional Kashmiri dress, is part of a unique cultural heritage that dates back centuries.

Dogri dance

Jammu is the heart of Dogra culture, which includes Dogri music, dance, and literature.

Kashmiri Handicrafts

The region is globally famous for Pashmina shawls, Kashmiri carpets, and Papier-mâché art.
