Shiv khori

Shiv Khori is a famous cave shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in the Reasi district of Jammu and Kashmir. It is one of the most revered pilgrimage sites in the region and draws thousands of devotees, especially during the festival of Mahashivratri.

Sanasar

Sanasar is a scenic hill station located about 20 km from Patnitop in the Udhampur district of Jammu and Kashmir. Named after two local lakes, Sana and Sar, the region is known for its lush green meadows, dense forests, and breathtaking views of the Himalayan mountain range.
