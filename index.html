Explore Districts of Jammu & Kashmir

JAMMU: A beautiful place with rich culture.

"Jammu is renowned for its majestic temples, historical palaces, and vibrant local culture. The city is often called the 'City of Temples' due to its numerous sacred shrines."

SRINAGAR: Known for its scenic beauty.

"Srinagar is renowned for its stunning Dal Lake, vibrant houseboats, and majestic Mughal gardens. The city's rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty attract tourists year-round."

REASI: A hub of historical landmarks.

"Reasi is a district known for its scenic landscapes, ancient temples, and adventurous activities. It is home to the holy Vaishno Devi Shrine, one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in India."

UDHAMPUR: Famous for its traditional festivals.

"Udhampur is renowned for its picturesque landscapes and rich cultural heritage. The district boasts beautiful attractions such as the Patnitop hill station, known for its green meadows."

KISHTWAR: Offers breathtaking landscapes.

"Kishtwar District is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes and serene beauty.Kishtwar also boasts the historical Kishtwar Fort and the revered Machail Mata Temple."

KATHUA: Known for its vibrant markets.

"Kathua, known as the 'Gateway to Jammu,' offers a rich tapestry of cultural and natural attractions.The district is home to the Ujh River and the Ranjit Sagar Dam."

POONCH: The unexplored heaven.

"Poonch District is Nestled in the Pir Panjal range, it offers stunning landscapes,and serene lakes.Poonch is also famous for its vibrant cultural festivals and traditional crafts."

DODA: A paradise for nature lovers.

"Doda district is home to the famous Bhagwati Temple and the serene Gandoh region. For those interested in trekking, Doda offers several scenic trails with panoramic views."

RAJOURI: Rich in historical heritage.

"Rajouri District is renowned for its breathtaking beauty.Key attractions include the Rajouri Fort, which offers panoramic views of the surrounding area, and the Nandani Wildlife Sanctuary.
